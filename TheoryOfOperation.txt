Theory of Operation
============

The I Ching Oracle is the opposite of modern physics.  In physics one tries to eliminate change and use
the "Law of Large Numbers" to get a probability, not an exact prediction, for a specific event.

In the I Ching, one places chance at the center to get one specific number in a unique time to interpret a
specific context.

The central idea is that there is one world [Unix Mundis*]. The psychic and physical are unified.

By asking the oracle a specific, clear, important question when one or one's group is under stress,
one's psychic energy activates the network of connections and the number which results from the
"casting" can be used to interpret the psycho-physical context which is the situation to be navigated.

The analogy is an electrical network where physical energy is applieed to light a bulb/LED in the circult.
Which bulb lights up indicats something of the, yes, current situation.
