Gritter Pattern

No mathematic explination has been found for the King Wen order of hexagrams.

Patterns here are "irrational" and accord to visual/symbolic structures.

Gert Gritter noticed a remarkable pattern in the hexagram pairs in 2014.

   https://www.yijing.nl/structures/Gritter.html

By rearranging the King Wen sequence around a central pillar consisting of the hexagram
pairs which are doubled or opposite trigrams, a visual, structural ordering of the pairs appears.

  6 - Old      Yin     [changing line]
  8 - Young Yin
  9 - Old      Yang  [changing line]
  7 - Young Yang

Note that one can combine figures 6+9 == 8+7 = 15
which is sum of "magic square" slice:
      4 9 2
      3 5 7
      8 1 6

A Jungian interpretation of the above would be a mandala of the Self meta-architype.
