Theory of Operation
===================

Operation of the I Ching Oracle is the opposite of modern physics.  
In physics one tries to eliminate change and use the
"Law of Large Numbers" to get a probability, not an exact prediction  
for a specific event.  

In the I Ching, one places chance at the center to get one specific 
number in a unique time to interpret a specific context.

Interestingly, in both physics and divination it is desired 
to eliminate consciousness (bias) from the experimental 
observation.

The fundamental idea is that THERE IS ONE WORLD [Unis Mundus*]. 
In us, experience of the psyche and the physical are unified.

By asking the oracle a specific, clear, important question when 
one or one's group is under stress, one's psychic energy activates 
the network of connections and the number which results from the 
"casting" can be used to interpret the psycho-physical context 
which is the situation to be navigated.

The analogy is an electrical network where physical energy is applied 
to light a bulb/LED in the circuit. Which bulb lights up indicates 
something of the, yes, current situation.

The psyche produces images with associated feeling intensity just as
we measure Voltage in an electric circuit. 

The connection between psyche and matter is Number, which  has aspects
both of quantity (physical measurement) and quality (psychological 
interpretation of numbers/hexagrams as living symbols).

The abstract way of thinking of this is that the chaos of the world
is given a field of number to yield orientation.  An example is that 
I am writing this on 7/March/2026 in the Northern Hemisphere.  This means 
that we are a transition into Spring with bird species migrating 
back from winter habitats, buds are swelling and opening, Coyotes are
making this year's den choices, and so forth.  My psyche is full of 
Spring images.  This date and location give a set of expected directions,
but again each year is different.  For example, at this latitude, we
have a Japanese Plum Tree which blossoms early.  If we get a "late frost"
it kills the blossoms and no plums are found.  Without a frost, we can look
forward to an early plum harvest.

So likewise, casting a hexagram and noting the changing lines is sampling
the current world situation.  Learning to "read" the "Language of the Lines"
takes some time to become fluent, but yields helpful image and direction.
